Bentota Turtle Hatchery

Bentota Turtle Hatchery Project is a popular attraction for all tourists in Bentota town. The pristine golden beaches that draw you to our island also provide a fascinating nesting ground for several endangered species of turtles, from the olive ridley to the leatherback turtle.

The purpose of this project is to take care of hatchery authorities until the sea turtles swim back to shore and lay their eggs until the hatchlings are ready to swim in the water. Preserving and protecting eggs from predators and other hazards. Visit the Bentho Turtle Hatchery to learn about these beautiful and endangered creatures of the sea.
